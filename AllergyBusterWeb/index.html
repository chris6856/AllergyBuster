You can't always rely on others to keep you safe.

When you have a food allergy, every meal is a risk assessment. Ingredient labels are dense and confusing. Restaurant staff may not know what's in the kitchen. Friends and family mean well but don't always have the answers.

For millions of people, this isn't inconvenient — it's dangerous. A single hidden allergen can mean anaphylaxis, an ER visit, or worse.

AllergyBuster puts the information in your hands, instantly, wherever you are. Scan a product in the grocery store aisle. Photograph an ingredient label at a dinner party. Search before you order. One action. Complete allergen information. No uncertainty.

Everything you need to keep safe while living your best life.
