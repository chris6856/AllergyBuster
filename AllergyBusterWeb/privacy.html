Privacy Policy

How AllergyBuster collects, uses, and protects your information.

Last updated: April 8, 2026

Overview

AllergyBuster ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you use the AllergyBuster mobile application and website (allergybuster.com), how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

We designed AllergyBuster with privacy in mind. We do not require account creation, we do not sell your data, and we do not track your individual scans or searches.

Information We Collect

Information you provide directly: If you submit restaurant allergen data through our website contribution form, we collect the information you enter: restaurant name, location, menu item details, allergen selections, and any notes you include. This data is submitted voluntarily to help improve the community database.

Device and usage information: Like most mobile apps and websites, we may collect basic technical information including device type, operating system version, app version, and general usage patterns (e.g., which features are used). This is used solely to improve app performance and stability.

Analytics: We may use privacy-respecting analytics tools to understand general usage trends — such as which app screens are visited most. This data is aggregated and does not identify individual users.

Crash reports: If the app crashes, an anonymized crash report may be sent to help us diagnose and fix the problem. These reports do not include the content of your searches or scans.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect or store the barcodes you scan
  • We do not collect or store photos you take within the app
  • We do not collect or store search terms you enter
  • We do not require account registration or collect login credentials
  • We do not collect your precise GPS location
  • We do not collect payment information

How We Use Information

Information collected is used only to:

  • Operate and improve the AllergyBuster app and website
  • Review and publish community-contributed restaurant allergen data
  • Diagnose technical issues and fix bugs
  • Understand aggregate usage to prioritize features

Third-Party Services

AllergyBuster uses the following third-party services to function:

  • Open Food Facts — an open-source food product database. Queries are made on your behalf but are not linked to your identity.
  • Anthropic Claude API — used to generate daily allergy tips. No personally identifiable information is sent to this service.
  • Cloudflare Workers — powers our backend API for restaurant allergen data. Cloudflare may log standard request metadata per their privacy policy.

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for advertising purposes.

Data Retention

Community-contributed restaurant allergen submissions are retained indefinitely to maintain the database. Crash reports and analytics data are retained for up to 90 days. We do not retain individual scan or search data because we do not collect it.

Children's Privacy

AllergyBuster is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has submitted information to us, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights including the right to access, correct, or delete information we hold about you. Since we collect minimal information, most users have nothing to request. If you submitted restaurant allergen data and wish to have it removed, please contact us at privacy@allergybuster.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of AllergyBuster after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@allergybuster.com or use the contact form on our website.
